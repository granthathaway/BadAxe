Wyrd

The Wyrd is one of my personal favorites. The antique finish on the blade is pretty sick. and the entire length of the handle is treated with a melted-in beeswax finish.

This also has the most detailed modifications to the axe head that I've done so far, and I think it turned out great. Nobody has told me otherwise, but I was also the one holding the axe when I asked their opinion.

So if you have this axe, people agree with you.
